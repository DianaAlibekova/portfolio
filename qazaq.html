QAZAQ STUDENT ASSOCIATION |2023

Diana is a member of the Qazaq Student Association at New York University Abu Dhabi, created by the initiation of her and 9 other Kazakhstani students. The organization is directed to intercultural exchange by presenting the Kazakh identity and traditions on an international platform.


Diana is responsible not only for the event organization but also for the creation and management of all the creative content. She designed physical posters about upcoming events attached to the walls of the university and all the social media digital content such as posts, stories, giveaways, videos, and edits as well as communicating with the audience.


In two weeks 300 students followed the Instagram account, which is around 16% of the student body at NYUAD and around 200 students attended the events which is more than 10% of the student body.
